Return Policy

Customers can return eligible items within 30 days of delivery. A return request must be initiated through the support portal or customer service channel.

After the return request is approved, a return label will be emailed to the customer. The customer must attach the return label to the package before sending it back.

Returned items must be unused, undamaged, and in original packaging. Items that are opened, damaged by the customer, or missing original packaging may not be eligible for return.

The assistant must clearly mention the return label when responding to return-related queries.
